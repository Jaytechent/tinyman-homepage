The Efficiency and Security of Algorand

Algorand is the blockchain that enables the creation of frictionless financial assets. By implementing their smart contracts at layer 1, Algorand makes pooling and swapping as safe and efficient as single-payment transactions on other blockchains. This allows Tinyman to offer the lowest transaction fees and allows contracts to resolve in seconds.
